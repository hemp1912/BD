Our Story

Decor that feels like home, built for the biggest day of your life

Started by the Deshpande family in a small Dadar workshop, now trusted across Mumbai.

Bhoomi began in 2012 when Rajesh and Meena Deshpande started building mandaps for relatives and neighbours. Word travelled fast — by 2016 we were decorating weddings across Mumbai, Thane and Navi Mumbai, blending traditional Maharashtrian and Gujarati motifs with whatever theme a couple dreamed of.

Today our in-house team of carpenters, florists, lighting technicians and designers handles everything from the haldi mandap to the sangeet stage — so your family can focus on the wedding, not the setup.

What We Do

Every element of your wedding decor, under one roof

From the mandap structure to the last fairy light, we plan, build and style each piece so it works together as one design — not six separate vendors.

01

Mandap Design & Build

Custom wooden and metal mandap structures — traditional four-pillar, floating canopy, or fusion designs — built to match your venue and theme.

02

Flower Decor

Fresh marigold, rose and orchid installations, or long-lasting artificial florals for outstation venues and budget-friendly options.

03

Lighting & Stage Setup

Warm fairy lights, uplighting, gobo projections and LED backdrops that change the mood from haldi morning to sangeet night.

04

Theme Weddings

Dedicated decor concepts for haldi, mehendi, sangeet and reception — colour-coded, photo-ready, and distinct from the main mandap.

05

Catering Tie-Up

Trusted catering partners for vegetarian, Jain and multi-cuisine wedding menus, coordinated with your decor timeline.

06

Full Venue Styling

Entrance arches, welcome decor, photo booths and seating design — so the whole venue feels considered, not just the stage.

How We Work

From first call to final petal, in four steps

01

Discovery Call

We learn your dates, venue, guest count, budget and the rituals you're planning for — usually a 20 minute call or visit.

02

Design & Moodboard

We share a mandap design, colour palette and a quote broken down by function (mandap, flowers, lighting, etc.) so there are no surprises.

03

Build & Setup

Our team arrives a day (or hours) before your event, builds the structure, and styles every detail before your first guest walks in.

04

Event Day & Teardown

A coordinator stays on-site through the event for touch-ups, then we handle full teardown — you don't lift a finger.
